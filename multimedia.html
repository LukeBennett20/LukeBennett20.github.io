Learn German Prepositions

AQA, OCR and Edexcel GCSE exam boards require GCSE students like you, to have basic knowledge of the German prepositions (Pr채positionen in German). Students who take the higher tier exam will almost definetely be required to use prepostions in the exam.

Reminder! What is a preposition?

Read the sentence below and listen to this short clip to find out what a preposotion is:

Prepositions are words which indicate location. They are used before a noun, a noun phrase, or a pronoun, connecting it to another word. For example, in the sentence 'The cat jumped over the garden fence', 'over' is the preposition.
