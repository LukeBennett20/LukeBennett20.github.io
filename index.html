Welcome to Genius German!.

This website will provide basic grammar exercises, vocabulary and information about German culture. This will help learners reach level A2 of the European Framework.

Learn about German Culture

We will provide information on German culture, and information on the biggest events in Germany such as Oktoberfest. We will also include elements of Swiss German and Austrian German. We provide cultural information culture elements such as: food, music and fashion. Get Germanized!

Learn Basic Grammar

We will teach you basic grammar such as adjective endings, word order and punctuation. Dont Worry! It will be easy.

Learn Basic Vocabulary

We will also provide basic vocabulary on items that you will use evrday: food and drink, directions, and school vocabulary.
