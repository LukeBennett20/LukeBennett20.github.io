Grammar: Perfect and Imperfect Tenses

This page will provide information and explanations about the perfect and imperfect tenses in German.

The Perfect Tense

You will have almost definetely come across the Perfect tense(das Perfekt in German), but you may not know exactly what it is. It is the most common tense used, and is particularly used in speech.
