Environment Vocaulary Video

The following video provides some extra vocabulary related to the environent. Not all of this vocab is required for your GCSE exam. This is just for those who would like to impress the examiner and challenge themselves.

Did You Know... To improve recycling rates, Germany intoduced a system named Pfandflasche ('Bottle deposits' in English). Pfand is portion of the price on a bottle of drink that you get back if you return the bottle to the retail chain you bought it from. This can be up to 0.25 euros per bottle! So, don't throw your plastic and alluminium bottles away when you got to Germany!

If you would like to learn more about this system then please visit the following website: PfandflascheThe author provides a very interesting account on how it helps the enviroment and the etiquette of leaving bottles at someones house in order for them to collect the Pfand (deposit) back.
